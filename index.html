My project

Last Meal Food Web

The Last Meal food web explains how plants, animals, and predators are connected through feeding. Plants provide food for herbivores like rabbits and insects. These herbivores become the “last meal” for carnivores such as snakes and birds. Finally, top predators like lions or eagles eat the carnivores. This cycle shows how every creature’s last meal helps keep the ecosystem alive and balanced.
